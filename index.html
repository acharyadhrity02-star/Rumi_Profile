Key R&D Projects

Bioactive Ion-Reinforced Bio-Ceramic Ink

Development of shear-thinning hybrid ink integrating bio-ceramics matrix with bioactive ions and polymeric binders for high-fidelity DIW printing with enhanced osteogenic and antibacterial performance.

Mg–Nanodiamond–HAp 3D-Printed Scaffold

Ion-releasing hybrid scaffold with controlled Mg²⁺ release (ICP-OES) and osteogenic validation (ALP/ARS/qPCR) for bone tissue engineering applications.

mCNC Conductive Microneedle Hydrogel

Dual-stimulus platform enhancing ZO-1/YAP-TAZ pathways for skin barrier restoration with tunable rheological behavior for smart skin patch applications.

Piezoelectric ZnO–Mg²⁺ Bioglass Scaffold

Piezoelectric-assisted bone regeneration with angiogenic enhancement for advanced tissue engineering applications.
